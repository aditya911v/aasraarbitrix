How It Works

Simple. Secure. Structured.

Step 1: Submit Your Dispute

Fill out our secure form with case details.Get instant acknowledgment via email.

Step 2: Neutral Is Assigned

We assign a trained neutral or allow party suggestion (if both agree).

Step 3: Scheduling & Consent

We reach out to both parties and fix a session time—no court visits needed.

Step 4: Online Proceedings Begin

The neutral conducts secure sessions via video, email, or chat.

Step 5: Resolution Agreement

The neutral drafts a legally binding settlement or award.

Step 6: Closure & Feedback

Parties get digital copies and provide feedback. Case is securely archived.
